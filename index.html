01 · About

Sales & application engineering, grounded in the shop floor.

Currently the Sales & Application Engineer at Workarm Engineering Services, an authorized representative for internationally recognized metal cutting tools and machineries. The work centers on understanding customer needs, planning sales and opportunities, and building long-term relationships built on mutual benefit.

Day to day, that means studying customer component drawings to establish the right process and planning, running demonstrations of new products and recording results to optimize cycle time, and staying close to competitor activity and market developments that affect the business. When something goes wrong at a customer site, resolving it — quickly and in person — is part of the job.

Before Workarm, managed sourcing at Bimal Auto Agency, Bangalore's largest Maruti Suzuki dealership, evaluating vehicles, quoting fair value, and closing and documenting orders.
